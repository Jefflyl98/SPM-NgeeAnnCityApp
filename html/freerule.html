Free Play Game Rules

In Free Play mode, this city-building game has an unlimited number of coins and begins with a grid of 5x5. In each turn, the player can construct any of the buildings in the city, on any cell in the city. Each construction costs 1 coin. Once a building is constructed along the border of the city, additional 5 more rows of cells are added to the perimeter of the city. The city will expand to 15x15 on the first expansion, and 25x25 on the second expansion.

A building is adjacent to another building if they are connected via the same road. The scoring of the buildings is the same as that in Arcade mode.

Profit and Upkeep Cost of Buildings

  • Residential (R): Each residential building generates 1 coin per turn. Each cluster of residential buildings (must be immediately next to each other) requires 1 coin per turn to upkeep.
  • Industry (I): Each industry generates 2 coins per turn and costs 1 coin per turn to upkeep.
  • Commercial (C): Each commercial generates 3 coins per turn and costs 2 coins per turn to upkeep.
  • Park (O): Each park costs 1 coin to upkeep.
  • Road (*): Each unconnected road segment costs 1 coin to upkeep.

Arcade Game Rules

In this city-building game, players start with 16 coins and build on a 20x20 grid. Each turn, players randomly select one of two buildings and construct it for 1 coin. Buildings must be placed next to existing structures after the first placement.

Types of Buildings and Scoring

  • Residential (R): Scores based on adjacency. Points for Residential (R), Commercial (C), and Parks (O).
  • Industry (I): Scores 1 point per industry in the city. Generates coins from adjacent Residential (R) buildings.
  • Commercial (C): Scores based on adjacent Commercial (C) buildings. Generates coins from adjacent Residential (R) buildings.
  • Park (O): Scores points based on adjacency to other Parks (O).
  • Road (*): Scores points based on connected roads in the same row.

Point System

  • Points are awarded for completing levels.
  • Bonus points for achieving specific goals.
  • Penalties for mistakes or failures.

Help - Game Rules

As the mayor of Ngee Ann City, your goal is to build the happiest and most prosperous city possible, scoring the most points.

There are 2 game modes: Arcade mode and Free Play mode.

Arcade Mode

In Arcade mode, the game begins with 16 coins. Each turn, you will construct one of two randomly selected buildings in the 20x20 city grid, costing 1 coin each. The first building can be placed anywhere, but subsequent buildings must be connected to existing ones. The building not chosen is discarded.

Free Play Mode

In Free Play mode, the game starts with unlimited coins and a 5x5 grid. Each turn, you can construct any building in the city on any cell, costing 1 coin each. When a building is placed along the city's border, the grid expands by 5 rows. The city will expand to 15x15 on the first expansion and 25x25 on the second.

A building is adjacent to another if they are connected via the same road. Scoring is the same as in Arcade mode.
